    H.E JOHN MWANIKI

    A graduate of Economics and Finance degree from St. Francis Xavier University in Canada, and the University of Technology, Sydney in 1994 and 1995 (master's degree), I am a seasoned economist and financial markets expert with over 24 years’ experience in leadership and management within diverse settings in Kenya, South Africa, Canada and Australia. An accomplished practitioner in the African financial markets, Mr. Muriithi has spearheaded numerous financial market strengthening initiatives across African countries covering establishment of credit sharing and scoring institutions, improved bond market function, expansion of mortgage markets, and even growth of women-owned enterprises. I was primarily involved in the design and implementation of these programs and contributed substantively to the development of financial markets that provide required services to many nations.

    I am a Technology and Financial Markets expert by profession with over 20 years’ experience in developing policy and solutions for the capital markets industry. My plan is to utilize my vast experience in general management, strategic planning as well as project management gained from implementing financial markets solutions in the Central Bank of South Sudan, Central Bank of Kenya, Central Bank of Seychelles, Nairobi Securities Exchange, Capital Markets Authority, Rwanda Capital Markets and SADC Inter-connectivity among others, to develop a roadmap for Laikipia County and make it an investment destination. My vision for Laikipia County is guided by the following key pillars of my manifesto.
